Our Services

Individual Counseling

Individual counseling offers personalized, one-on-one support to address your specific mental health concerns, providing a safe space to explore feelings, develop coping strategies, and achieve personal growth.

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
  • Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT)

Group Therapy

Group therapy provides a supportive environment where individuals share experiences and learn from each other, fostering a sense of community, understanding, and mutual growth in addressing common mental health challenges.

  • Support Groups
  • Psychoeducational Groups
  • Interpersonal Process Groups

Online Counseling

Online counseling offers convenient and confidential mental health support through virtual sessions, allowing you to receive professional guidance and support from the comfort and privacy of your own home.

  • Teletherapy Sessions
  • Virtual CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)
  • E-Therapy Chat Counseling
