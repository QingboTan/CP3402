Reflection and Insights

Describe your experience using the two different CMSs.
What have you learned through this assignment regarding the different systems?

In the beginning of this project, I encountered several challenges while trying to follow instructions related to local development areas, particularly with version and compatibility issues. Despite these setbacks, I persevered and opted for a different method that I was more familiar with. By updating my knowledge and utilizing the latest software versions, I successfully created a local development environment. Uploading the work to the hosting platform then became a straightforward process.

For the WordPress aspect of the project, I developed the website entirely online and updated the content based on the same information provided by the gym. Comparing both CMSs, I found that the choice of theme can significantly impact the project. However, WordPress offers simpler and clearer ways to access various parts of the website. For example, managing menus is much easier in WordPress compared to Joomla.

Through this assignment, I learned that while both WordPress and Joomla have their strengths, WordPress tends to offer a more intuitive and user-friendly experience, especially for tasks such as managing menus and other website components. The choice of theme is crucial in both systems and can greatly influence the overall ease of use and functionality of the website.
